Our Honor Society

Founded in 1885 at Lehigh University, Tau Beta Pi is the oldest engineering honor society in the United States. There are now collegiate chapters at 250 US colleges and universities, 47 active alumni chapters in 16 districts across the country, and a total initiated membership of approximately 632,000.

Visit the national website for more information about the organization.

Our Chapter

The Texas Xi chapter was officially installed on February 15th, 2020. Our chapter strives to promote future growth by nurturing integrity, character, and service.

As a new chapter, our current objective is to increase awareness of the chapter on campus. Learn how we are doing this by looking at our upcoming events.
