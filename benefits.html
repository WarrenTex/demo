Benefits

When you become a member of Tau Beta Pi, you gain access to exclusive perks. See the official site for a full list.

  • Scholarships

    Each year, Tau Beta Pi awards at least 250 scholarships of $2,000 to undergraduate members for their senior year of engineering.

    In addition, 30 fellowships of $10,000 are awarded to graduate students for one year in any engineering discipline.

  • Professional Development

    Through the award-winning Engineering Futures program, members can learn more about the teamwork, problem-solving, ethics, inclusion, management, and interpersonal and presentation skills needed to be successful in their careers.

  • The Bent Magazine

    A 4-year subscription to Tau Beta Pi's quarterly magazine is included in the membership fee.

  • Networking & Mentoring

    With 250 active chapters, there are many opportunities available for members to connect with each other and alumni.

  • Additional Benefits

    Members receive discounts on Geico Insurance, Dell Technologies products, and more.
